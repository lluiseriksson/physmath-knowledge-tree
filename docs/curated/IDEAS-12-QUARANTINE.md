# Quarantine — ideas from `ideas(12).txt`

Quarantine preserves potentially useful concepts without presenting them as established mathematics or contaminating the main research graph.

## Uniform SUSY decoupling to pure Yang-Mills

A useful theorem would need a polymer-profile error tending to zero uniformly in volume, cutoff, RG scale, background and hole geometry. No such bridge is supplied in the source. Failure condition: the auxiliary determinant leaves unmatched gauge counterterms, phases or normalization factors.

## Local nonperturbative Nicolai map

An invertible, local or exponentially localized map with exact Jacobian cancellation would be highly consequential, but the source contains only an interface proposal. Failure condition: the map is perturbative, nonlocal, noninvertible or does not preserve the pure gauge measure.

## Periodic-table and closed-super-shell analogy

The dimensions 2, 6, 10 and 14 follow from orbital degeneracy with spin. The proposed transfer to RG shell cancellation is an analogy, not a theorem. Failure condition: no background-covariant shell decomposition and odd pairing operator produce a smaller polymer majorant.

## Petz recovery and geometric portals

Petz recovery is a mathematically meaningful reconstruction channel. It does not by itself transport matter or create spacetime geometry. The portal and wormhole interpretation is excluded until a physical dilation, causal spacetime model and stress-energy solution are provided.

## RAP and projective carrying

The source proposes carrying a reduced defect through a polymer tree rather than summing positive weights. This remains a research sketch until an operator, norm, contraction inequality and downstream comparison theorem are defined.

## Hodge–Mayer–Ward theory

The elementary transgression identity can be separated from a broader proposed theory. The latter remains speculative until a concrete local contraction, cohomology splitting and non-linear Mayer compatibility theorem are proved on the actual activity space.
