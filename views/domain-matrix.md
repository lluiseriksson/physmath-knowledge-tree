# Domain Matrix

Rows are source domains. Columns are moves. Cells are candidate research
directions.

| Source | Spectralize | Categorify | Geometrize | Probabilize | Renormalize |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Number theory | Explicit formulas, zeros as spectra | Motives and sheaves | Arithmetic geometry | Random Euler products | Prime-scale flows |
| PDE | Operators and gaps | Sheaves of local solutions | Flows and curvature | Stochastic PDE | Critical cascades |
| Gauge theory | Mass gap as spectral gap | Moduli stacks | Connections and curvature | Lattice measures | Scale flow |
| Complexity | Spectra of operators | Resource categories | Orbit closures | Random restrictions | Barrier flow |
| Algebraic geometry | Hodge Laplacians | Derived categories | Moduli spaces | Random varieties | Degeneration limits |

## Reading a cell

Each cell should become either:

- a bridge node,
- an edge with mechanism,
- a falsified route,
- or a Lean toy target.
